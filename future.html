Future Vision

Designing a Future Where Sport Meets Tech

I picture Kenyan girls tying their skates as easily as opening a learning app. I plan to use user research, sport ideas, and clear stories to make that real.

Short-term (1‑2 years)

  • Finish my Zetech BBIT capstone on wearable Roll Ball sensors and test the ideas with Kingdom Bank innovation squads.
  • Coach Meru county girls so they qualify for nationals.
  • Publish two case studies in both English and Swahili.

Mid-term (3‑5 years)

  • Take graduate studies in Human-Computer Interaction.
  • Start a sports-tech studio for African women builders.
  • Release an open API for Kenyan Roll Ball statistics.

Long-term Vision

Build an East African lab where planners, skaters, and technologists test safer recreation spaces.

Each page on this site is both a story and a plan toward that goal.
