Roll Ball Medalist · Designer · Project Manager

Hey, I’m Winnie Mutembei.

I grew up in Meru, studied my primary years at Chogoria Boarding, and later attended Chogoria Girls High School. Today I manage projects at Kingdom Bank while studying Bachelor of Business Information Technology (BBIT) at Zetech University. I build digital tools during the day and skate for Team Kenya Roll Ball at night. This site keeps a simple record of that journey.
