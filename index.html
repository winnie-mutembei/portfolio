Hey, I’m Winnie Mutembei.

I was born in Meru and raised between tea estates and packed dorm halls at Chogoria Boarding. Today I’m a Zetech University Information Technology student, co-creating digital products by day and skating for Team Kenya Roll Ball by night. This site is my living archive of movement—across towns, classrooms, and global arenas.
