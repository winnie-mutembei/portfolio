Chapter 2

Learning in Lockstep with Skates

Every classroom has been a training camp for curiosity. From Chogoria Boarding to Zetech University, I’ve balanced lab reports, multimedia projects, and national team practice. Scholarships, stubborn mentors, and community Wi-Fi hubs made this path possible.

Academic Journey

Chogoria Boarding Primary (2010‑2016) Prefect for the music and athletics clubs, topped Meru North science contests, and coded my first Scratch story in the computer lab.
Chogoria Girls High School (2017‑2020) Served as wellness captain, launched a Roll Ball mentorship program, and built an SMS homework reminder for boarders.
Zetech University (2021‑Present) BSc in Information Technology, GPA 3.74/4.0, specializing in UX research, sport analytics, and immersive media storytelling.

Extracurricular Highlights

  • Captain, Zetech Roll Ball club; organized bi-county friendlies.
  • Google Developer Student Clubs research mentor (2023 cohort).
  • Resident assistant for Meru students adjusting to city life.

Academic Projects

  • SpinTrack: Wearable analytics dashboard capturing skate cadence and injury risks.
  • Meru Memory Map: Interactive site highlighting women's sports pioneers from Eastern Kenya.
  • Chogoria Voices: NLP bot answering parent queries about school policies in English and Kimeru.

Learning Stack

Technical Coursework

Algorithms, database systems, web security, UX research methods.

Humanities Electives

Sports psychology, East African literature, ethics of AI, storytelling.

Professional Badges

IBM Enterprise Design Thinking, Google UX Design, FIH SportSafe.

How Education Shapes My Work

  • Roll Ball statistics projects sharpen my love for inclusive sport tech.
  • Storytelling electives help me document athletes beyond medals.
  • Leadership practicums ensure my teams stay grounded in service.
