Education Journey

Learning in Lockstep with Skates

Every school has felt like a training ground. From Chogoria Boarding to Zetech University, where I’m taking Bachelor of Business Information Technology (BBIT), I have balanced class work, media projects, and national team training. Scholarships, patient mentors, and shared Wi-Fi hubs kept me moving.

Academic Journey

Chogoria Boarding Primary (2010‑2016) Led the music and athletics clubs, won Meru North science contests, and coded my first Scratch story in the computer lab.
Chogoria Girls High School (2017‑2020) Served as wellness captain, started a Roll Ball mentorship group, and built an SMS homework reminder for boarders.
Zetech University (2021‑Present) Bachelor of Business Information Technology (BBIT), GPA 3.74/4.0, specializing in UX research, sport analytics, and immersive media storytelling.

Extracurricular Highlights

  • Captain, Zetech Roll Ball club; organized friendly games.
  • Google Developer Student Clubs research mentor (2023).
  • Resident assistant for Meru students settling into city life.

Academic Projects

  • SpinTrack: Wearable dashboard that tracks skate speed and injury risks.
  • Meru Memory Map: Interactive site that highlights women sports pioneers from Eastern Kenya.
  • Chogoria Voices: Chat bot that answers school policy questions in English and Kimeru.

Learning Stack

Technical Coursework

Algorithms, databases, web security, UX research methods.

Humanities Electives

Sports psychology, East African literature, AI ethics, storytelling.

Professional Badges

IBM Enterprise Design Thinking, Google UX Design, FIH SportSafe.

How Education Shapes My Work

  • Roll Ball data projects push me to build tools for everyone.
  • Story classes help me talk about athletes beyond medals.
  • Leadership practice keeps my teams focused on service.
